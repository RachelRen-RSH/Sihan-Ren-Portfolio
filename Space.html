Welcome to the Era of Commercial Space



At the beginning of 21st Century, the commercial space industry becomes the leading of space launch and exploration system. In order to analyze the history, the current situation and the future of its development, the story line is built through ArcGIS Story Map with three kinds of map components including a static thematic map of the overview of current space launch industry, a map tour of commercial space companies in America, and two ArcGIS Online maps of flight influences and worldwide commercial space companies. The story proposes three problems to solve and discusses about possible solutions with advantages and drawbacks.
