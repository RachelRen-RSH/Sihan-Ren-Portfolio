The Sweet Killer



When you are standing in front of a beverage vending machine, will you choose a bottle of water or a can of sweetened soda? The map “The Sweet Killer” is made through ArcGIS Story Map and discusses the condition of obesity and its relation to consuming sugar beverage in US during 2013 and 2017. The story includes a bivariate thematic map with choropleth symbols to show the severity of obesity and bar symbols to compare the sweetened beverages consuming rate. Two states, Iowa and District of Columbia, are used to represent the inverse relationship. The story map is supposed to appeal people to reducing the sugar beverage consumption in order to maintain a healthy lifestyle and get rid of fatal diseases.
