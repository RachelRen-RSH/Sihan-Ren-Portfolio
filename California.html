California Covid-19 Homeless Impact



At the beginning of 2020, COVID-19 pandemic broke out around the world. As people concern about the daily increased cases, related social issues generated accordingly. Because most people with low salaries are forced to lose their jobs, it is hard for them to pay for the house rent, which causes a serious homeless impact. In order to appeal more people to care more about such issues, the map compares the homeless condition in California impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic by county. To show the relationship between the control of COVID-19 and the help for homeless people, there are two days, one is July 23, 2020 and another is April 6, 2021, which can represent the condition in the first peak of increased cases and the current condition which the situation turned better.
