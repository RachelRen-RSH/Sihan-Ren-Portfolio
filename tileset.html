Children's Drawing of the World



Can children become cartographers? The answer is YES! Children love using crayons and color pencils to draw their own view of the beautiful world. This tileset web map uses Mapbox studio to create a children’s drawings’ theme with handwriting types, crayon’s textures, bright colors and ordinal form when zooming into different scales. While zooming into Madison, eight icons represent places that children often go, including schools, libraries, museums, amusement parks, shopping malls, hamburger shops, parks and dog parks.
